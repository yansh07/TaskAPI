Under The Hood

We didn't just use `list.append()`. We engineered it.

Priority Queues

We use Python's collections.deque for O(1) push/pop operations. Your high-priority tasks never wait in line.

RBAC & Security

JWT Authentication and Role-Based Access Control baked in. Admins rule, users drool, and read-only accounts just watch.

Async Workers

Heavy lifting happens in the background. Emails, webhooks, and notifications run on Celery/Redis without blocking your main thread.

Data Export

Need your data? Grab it in JSON or CSV instantly. We don't hold your tasks hostage.

Rate Limiting

Integrated FastAPI-Limiter ensures your API doesn't crash when someone writes a `while True` loop against your endpoint.

DB Agnostic

Currently rocking SQLite for dev speed, but SQLAlchemy ORM means switching to Postgres is just a connection string away.
